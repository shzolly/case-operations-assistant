# Civil Hearing Scheduling Guidance

Source ID: SOP-CIV-003
Domain: Civil

When a civil case is awaiting a hearing date, staff should confirm that the
answer or responsive pleading has been filed and that the case is ready for
scheduling.

If the last docket event occurred within the normal scheduling window, no
overdue follow-up is required. Staff should continue monitoring the case queue
according to division procedure.

Scheduling notices must be reviewed before they are sent externally.
