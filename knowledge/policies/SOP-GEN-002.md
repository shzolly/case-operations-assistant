# Sensitive Case Action Approval

Source ID: SOP-GEN-002
Domain: General Operations

Actions that create official notices, change case disposition, route filings
for judicial review, close or reopen a case, or send external communications
must be approved by a supervisor before execution.

The approval record must include the case id, requesting user, proposed action,
supporting rationale, approval decision, deciding user, and decision timestamp.

The assistant may draft a sensitive action, but it must not execute the action
directly.
